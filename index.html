Millions of individuals in communities all around the world have little to no access to education.

Immediate access to resources to learn anything you’re curious about is readily available in most of the developed world, but for individuals in underdeveloped areas, access to schooling and educational resources are scarce. It’s important not to take for granted the ability to receive an education, and with this piece of knowledge in mind, it’s up to us to provide support for those who need it.

WHAT COULD BE THE CAUSE?

Poverty serves as a main cause for this issue, and in areas where individuals face unemployment, education isn’t the first priority even if it is desired. When governments in poverty ridden areas don’t have the funding to build facilities such as schools and libraries, or recruit and train educators, it’s hard to establish a good schooling system. The digital divide also plays a role, where the lack of access to the internet and technology severely limits and restricts opportunities for learning.
